### Key Decisions
- **Carryover approach** — combine window grouping with standalone carryover (instead of separate post-backfill pass). Simpler, more efficient.
- **Summary generation timing** — only at thread creation via LLM, NOT refreshed when centroid-matched articles are added (cost optimization). Summary can become stale over time but will be re-evaluated in Phase 4.
- **Window re-match** — before LLM grouping, carryover standalones are re-tried against current threads (catches late joins). This improves threading rate without extra LLM cost.
- **Summary focus** — story progression chronological order, emphasize recent articles (not just similarity).
