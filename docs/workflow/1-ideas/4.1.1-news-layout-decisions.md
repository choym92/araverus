- "All" (default): all clusters shown
- Click "Markets": only clusters containing Markets articles shown, keyword bar updates to Markets-only keywords
- Click "Markets" + "Fed" keyword: clusters containing Markets articles tagged with "Fed"
- Categories and keywords are combinable, not exclusive

**Why not separate views/tabs for categories:**
- Same data, same articles â€” just different lens
- Users who want traditional browsing can use category filter + ignore clusters
- Users who want topic exploration can use keyword filter + clusters
- One system, no context switching
